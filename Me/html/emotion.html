HOW TO REMOVE NEGATIVE EMOTIONS

________________

“You are never going to get over it, but you'll get used to it. Just let yourself feel what you need to feel. Even if it hurts.”

We humans are often governed by emotions or in other words emotions will control our perception and behavior. Negative or positive emotions will lead to different behaviors. When we are positive, we work with joy and excitement. And vice versa when negative, people will see everything in sadness, dissatisfaction. So what should we do about negative emotions so that they don't have unfortunate consequences for our work and relationships?

First you have to believe that all your feelings are legitimate. Whether happy, sad, angry or hurt, tell yourself that you should feel this way. However, that does not mean that you indulge your emotions, let them be exaggerated and adversely affect your life. We admit to compromise and appease our own emotions.

Second, look at your feelings with objective eyes. Position yourself as an outsider to see things. Because the more you think, the more obsessed with it, the more difficult it will be to get out.

Third, try to be active (exercise, walk, clean, organize, etc.) or do whatever you feel like doing (watch a movie, eat some delicious food, talk to a friend) friends) as long as it ensures that your body is functioning and releasing energy. Using physical activity to release negative emotions is a very effective way. Because the main purpose is to shift your attention to another object from which to consciously express emotions.

Fourth, it is necessary to keep a distance from objects that can cause us negative emotions.

If you do not know how to control emotions but only suppress them, they will accumulate and pus. At some point, it will explode into disease or extreme actions. Knowing how to communicate your feelings will help you share it without inadvertently subjecting others to your emotional tolerance. Each person will have their own way to release negative emotions in a healthy way. Try it out and find the one that works best for you!

Don't let a bad day make your whole life meaningless.
